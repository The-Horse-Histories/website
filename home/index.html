About The Book

*The Mars Mystery*
by Austin Rice

In the harsh, unforgiving landscape of Mars, three scientists—Lucas, Marie, and Allison—embark on the Aurora Nine mission, their sights set on uncovering the mysteries left by their predecessors...

*The Mars Mystery* blends suspense, science fiction, and the thrill of discovery in a journey that asks whether humanity is ready to face what lies beyond our world—and within ourselves.
