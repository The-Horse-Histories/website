About the Book

*The Horse Histories* follows the journey of a young girl, Eliza, as she uncovers the stories of legendary horses throughout history. From the famous steeds of the war to the beloved companion of explorers, Eliza discovers that the bond between horses and humans is deeper than any history book could tell.
